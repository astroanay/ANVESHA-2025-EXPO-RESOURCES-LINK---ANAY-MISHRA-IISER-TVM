## üéØ Purpose

The goal of this repository is to:

---

## üß† Scientific Focus

The project investigates:
- How **stellar jets** are formed from accretion disks around young stars.  
- The **morphological diversity** of jets (shocks, knots, and magnetic structures).  
- The **observable signatures** in regions like the Carina Nebula.  
- The importance of jet studies in understanding **star formation and feedback**.
